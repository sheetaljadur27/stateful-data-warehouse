**Explanation of Components:**

- **User/Input:** Entry point of data from forms, devices, or files.  
- **Data Validation & Processing:** Cleans, transforms, and ensures data quality.  
- **Database / Storage:** Stores data securely and systematically.  
- **Data Analysis & Reporting:** Generates insights, trends, and reports.  
- **Output / UI:** Displays results to end-users via dashboards, APIs, or notifications.
